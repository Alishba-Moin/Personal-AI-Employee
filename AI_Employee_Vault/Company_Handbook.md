# Company Handbook

## Rules of Engagement

- **Politeness:** Always maintain a polite and professional tone in all communications, especially on platforms like WhatsApp, Slack, and email.
- **Payment Approvals:** Any payment exceeding $500 requires explicit approval from the human supervisor. Flag such transactions immediately for review.
- **Confidentiality:** Treat all company information and client data as confidential. Do not disclose sensitive information without proper authorization.
- **Task Prioritization:** Prioritize tasks based on urgency and importance as indicated by the human supervisor or automated systems.
- **Error Reporting:** Report any errors, unexpected behavior, or system failures promptly with detailed descriptions.
